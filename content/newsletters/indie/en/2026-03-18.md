## 🏆 Cover Story: Replit Raises $400M, Triples to $9B in Six Months

## 📊 Trend Signals

1. **Vibe coding went from tool to industry.** Six startups raising billions, Cursor at $2B ARR, Replit at $9B. Q1 2026 is the iPhone moment for this space.

2. **Acquire > Create is accelerating.** Noosa Labs ($120K MRR via micro-SaaS acquisitions) and Base44 ($80M exit after 6 months) prove that "buy and grow" works just as well in the AI era.

3. **Model releases are relentless.** GPT-5.4 mini/nano + Anthropic Marketplace + Meta delay + Nvidia GTC — all in one week. For indie hackers: **don't lock into a single model provider.** Flexibility is your moat.

4. **Product Hunt ≠ revenue.** BrandingStudio.ai (400 signups → 1 customer) confirms what many suspected: PH is awareness, not acquisition — especially for premium products. LinkedIn, SEO, and community still win.

5. **"Can't code" is no longer a barrier.** Jonathan Chan (ex-consultant, non-technical) and Business Insider's "vibe coding as a job" piece both point to the same thing — the barrier to building a software business drops by the month.
