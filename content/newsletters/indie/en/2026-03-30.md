## 🎯 Takeaway

1. **Platform risk is real.** Sora went from standalone app to dead in 6 months. Never build your core business on someone else's API without a Plan B.
2. **AI coding tools are going autonomous.** Claude Code Auto-fix, Cursor Agent Mode, Replit Agent — the 2026 trend is shifting from "AI-assisted" to "AI-executed."
3. **The solo builder ceiling keeps rising.** Pieter Levels at $3.5M ARR solo, Indian devs at $18K MRR with zero team — when tools are powerful enough, team size stops being a moat.
4. **SaaS valuation logic is being rewritten.** Public SaaS is getting crushed, but bootstrapped micro-SaaS is in its best era — you don't need funding to build AI-native.
5. **Mythos is coming.** Whether you use Claude or not, the next-gen capability jump will reshape every AI product's competitive landscape. Better to pay attention now than get blindsided later.
