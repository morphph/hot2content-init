## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: Mixture of Experts (MoE)

Several of today's trending models — Qwen3.5-35B-A3B, Qwen3.5-397B-A17B, LiquidAI LFM2-24B-A2B — use **Mixture of Experts** architecture. Here's what that means:

A standard model runs every parameter for every input. An MoE model has multiple "expert" sub-networks and a router that selects only a few experts per input. The "A3B" in Qwen3.5-35B-A3B means only 3B parameters are *active* per token, even though the total model is 35B.

**Why it matters:** You get the quality of a large model with the inference cost of a small one. This is why Qwen can release a 397B model that's actually practical to run — only 17B parameters activate per token.
