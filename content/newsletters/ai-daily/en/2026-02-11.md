## üéì MODEL LITERACY

**What is "inference speed" and why does it matter?** When you chat with an AI, the model has to "think" through billions of calculations to generate each word. Inference speed is how fast it does this. OpenAI just made GPT-5.2 40% faster without changing the model itself‚Äîthey optimized the computing infrastructure. Faster inference means lower costs, snappier responses, and the ability to tackle longer, more complex tasks without timing out.
