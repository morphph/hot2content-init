## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: What Is "Infrastructure Noise" in Benchmarks?

When you see Model A score 72% and Model B score 69% on a coding benchmark, you might assume A is clearly better. But Anthropic's new research shows that just changing infrastructure configuration — things like timeout settings, container specs, retry policies, and network latency — can swing scores by several percentage points. That's often larger than the gap between top models on leaderboards. This means many "Model X beats Model Y" claims might just be measuring whose test infrastructure was configured more favorably. Next time you see benchmark results, ask: did they control for infrastructure, or are you looking at noise?
