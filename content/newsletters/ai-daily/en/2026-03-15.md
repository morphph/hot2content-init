## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: Mixture of Experts (MoE)

This week's trending models highlight a pattern: **Mixture of Experts** architecture. NVIDIA's Nemotron 3 120B-A12B has 120 billion total parameters but only activates 12 billion per inference. Qwen 3.5's 35B-A3B model similarly activates just 3B of its 35B parameters.

**How it works:** Instead of one giant network, MoE models contain multiple specialized "expert" sub-networks. A lightweight "router" network decides which experts to activate for each input token. You get the knowledge of a huge model with the speed of a small one.

**Why it matters:** MoE makes frontier-quality models runnable on consumer hardware. A 120B-parameter MoE model can be faster than a dense 12B model while being smarter — because it has access to specialized knowledge distributed across all its experts.
