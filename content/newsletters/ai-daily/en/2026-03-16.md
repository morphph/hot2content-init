## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: What Is a Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) Model?

Qwen 3.5-35B-A3B has "A3B" in its name — that means it's a 35 billion parameter model that only **activates 3 billion** parameters per token. This is a Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture.

Think of it like a company with 35 specialists, but each question only goes to 3 of them. The rest stay idle. This means you get the knowledge of a 35B model at roughly the compute cost of a 3B model. It's why MoE models are dominating efficiency leaderboards — big brains, small compute bills.

HuggingFace just published a comprehensive guide on MoE in Transformers with 132 likes.
[Read more →](https://huggingface.co/blog/moe-transformers)
