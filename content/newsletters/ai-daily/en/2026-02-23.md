## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: What Are CUDA Kernels?

When HuggingFace says "AI agents can write custom CUDA kernels," they mean AI can now write the ultra-low-level GPU instructions that make models run fast. Think of CUDA kernels as recipes that tell each of the thousands of cores in your GPU exactly what math to do, in what order, using which memory. Writing them used to require years of expertise in parallel computing. Now, Claude and Codex can generate them from a description. This is significant because inference speed and cost are often bottlenecked by kernel efficiency.
