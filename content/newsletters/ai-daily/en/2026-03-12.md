## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: Tool Search

**What is Tool Search?** When AI models have access to many tools (APIs, functions), loading all tool descriptions into the prompt wastes tokens and can confuse the model. Tool search lets the model defer discovering available tools until runtime — it first understands what you want, then searches for the right tool. Think of it like a craftsperson who knows which drawer to open, rather than dumping every tool on the workbench. OpenAI just shipped this in GPT-5.4, and it's a pattern that will become standard for complex agent architectures.
