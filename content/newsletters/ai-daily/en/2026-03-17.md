## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: What Is an Agent Harness?

You'll see "agent harness" mentioned in Anthropic's engineering posts and across trending repos this week. An agent harness is the scaffolding that wraps around an AI model to turn it into a functional agent — handling things like tool use, memory persistence, error recovery, and multi-step task management.

Think of the model as an engine and the harness as the car around it. The engine generates text; the harness decides when to call tools, how to recover when something fails, and how to manage context across long-running tasks. Anthropic's research shows that harness design can matter more than model choice — a well-harnessed weaker model can outperform a poorly-harnessed stronger one.

The explosion of OpenClaw alternatives this week (nanobot, zeroclaw, ironclaw) is essentially a competition over who builds the best harness.
