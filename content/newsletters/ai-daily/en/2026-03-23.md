## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: Agentic Evals (and Why Benchmarks Lie)

Today's five Anthropic papers share a theme: evaluating AI agents is fundamentally harder than evaluating chatbots.

**The problem:** When you benchmark a model on a fixed test, you control the environment. When you benchmark an agent, the infrastructure it runs on — timeout settings, container specs, network latency — can swing scores by several percentage points. That's often larger than the gap between models on the leaderboard.

**Why it matters:** Every "Model X beats Model Y" claim on agentic benchmarks should come with an asterisk. The same model can score differently depending on how much RAM its sandbox has or how fast its API calls return. Before picking a model for your agent stack, test it in *your* infrastructure, not someone else's benchmark.
