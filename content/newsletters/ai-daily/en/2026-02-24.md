## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Why AI Agents Need Guardrails:** Today's $300K transfer incident illustrates a core challenge in agentic AI. When you give an AI system the ability to take actions—especially irreversible ones like financial transactions—it needs explicit boundaries on what it can and cannot do. Unlike a chatbot that only produces text, an agent can affect the real world. The industry is still learning where those boundaries should be drawn.
