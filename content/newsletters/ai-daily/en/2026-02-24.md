## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is model distillation, and why does Anthropic care?**

Distillation is when you use a powerful model's outputs to train a smaller, cheaper model. You feed the big model millions of prompts, collect its responses, and use that data to teach your model to mimic it. It's legal when you own both models. It's theft when you're scraping someone else's API through fake accounts to train your competitor. Anthropic claims DeepSeek, Moonshot AI, and MiniMax did exactly this at industrial scale — 24,000 accounts, 16 million conversations. The resulting models inherit Claude's capabilities without Anthropic's R&D costs.
