## üéì MODEL LITERACY: Mixture of Experts (MoE)
Qwen's new 397B model only activates 17B parameters per query ‚Äî that's **Mixture of Experts (MoE)**. Instead of running all 397B parameters, the model routes each input to a small subset of specialized "expert" sub-networks. Result: you get the quality of a huge model with the speed and cost of a small one. It's why models keep getting "bigger" on paper but don't get proportionally slower.
