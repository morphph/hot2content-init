## ğŸ“ MODEL LITERACY: Mixture-of-Experts (MoE)

Qwen3.5-397B-A17B uses MoE â€” what does that mean?

A regular model activates ALL its parameters for every token. A MoE model has many "expert" sub-networks but only activates a few for each token. Qwen3.5 has 397B total parameters but only uses 17B per token (the "A17B" part).

**Why it matters:** You get the knowledge of a 397B model at the compute cost of a 17B model. It's like having 20 specialists on call but only consulting 2-3 per question.
