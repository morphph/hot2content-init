## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY: Distillation Attacks

**What is model distillation?** It's a technique where you use a powerful model (the "teacher") to generate training data for a smaller model (the "student"). By sending carefully crafted prompts to Claude and collecting the responses, a lab can effectively transfer Claude's capabilities into their own model — like photocopying someone's brain, one question at a time. Anthropic says DeepSeek, Moonshot, and MiniMax did this at industrial scale (16M+ exchanges), which is why they've now added detection systems and banned the associated accounts.
