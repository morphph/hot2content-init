## 🏆 Cover Story: Anthropic Invades the Office Suite

Anthropic just launched [Claude Cowork & Plugins for the Enterprise](https://claude.com/blog/cowork-plugins-across-enterprise), embedding Claude directly into Excel, PowerPoint, Slack, Google Workspace, DocuSign, FactSet, and S&P Global. Not as a sidebar chatbot — as a native participant in your workflow, carrying context across applications.

This is the most direct challenge yet to Microsoft 365 Copilot and OpenAI's Frontier. [Business Insider](https://www.businessinsider.com/anthropic-ai-software-claude-microsoft-powerpoint-excel-slack-2026-2) calls it an "escalating AI battle." [CNN reports](https://www.cnn.com/2026/02/24/tech/anthropic-claude-plugins-office-jobs) Claude can now handle end-to-end multi-step tasks for HR, design, and wealth management roles.

Anthropic's framing: "In 2025, Claude Code transformed how software gets built. In 2026, we're bringing that transformation to all knowledge work."

## 🎯 Takeaways

1. **The office suite is now an AI battleground.** Anthropic going native in Excel/PPT/Slack is the first real threat to Microsoft Copilot's enterprise position.
2. **Model tiers are compressing.** Sonnet 4.6 approaches previous-gen Opus performance. For most developers, the price/performance frontier matters more than raw capability.
3. **OpenAI is fighting on every front** — IPO prep, hardware, ad monetization, model iteration. The GPT-4o petition shows that user loyalty is a variable they can't ignore.
4. **Apple conceded on AI models**, choosing Google Gemini to power Siri. The signal: model capability now trumps ecosystem control.
5. **February 2026 is the most intense month of AI model competition yet.** Three major labs shipping simultaneously, with DeepSeek V4 poised to add another shockwave.
