## 🏆 Cover Story: Microsoft Puts Claude Inside Copilot

The biggest product move this week: Microsoft launched **M365 Copilot Wave 3**, headlined by **Copilot Cowork** — built on Anthropic's Claude technology, integrated directly into the Copilot experience.

Microsoft is going all-in on multi-model. Claude is now available in mainline Copilot chat alongside OpenAI models, with the system choosing the best model per task. A new **E7 licensing tier** targets enterprise customers who want frontier capabilities with enterprise governance.

The signal is clear: Copilot is no longer an OpenAI-only product. Microsoft is positioning itself as the orchestration layer that transcends any single model provider.

## 🤖 Model & API Updates

**OpenAI: GPT-5.1 Officially Retired**
As of March 11, GPT-5.1 models (Instant, Thinking, Pro) have been removed from ChatGPT. Existing conversations automatically migrate to GPT-5.3 Instant / GPT-5.4 Thinking / GPT-5.4 Pro. OpenAI also shipped **interactive visual learning** — 70+ math and science concepts with manipulable visualizations.

🔗 [OpenAI Updates](https://releasebot.io/updates/openai)

**OpenAI × AWS: Trainium Chip Adoption**
OpenAI announced it will use AWS's custom Trainium chips for AI training. A major win for Amazon, and a subtle diversification away from NVIDIA dependency.

🔗 [Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/investing/2026/03/10/openai-gave-amazing-news-to-amazon-shareholders/)

## 🏢 Platform Moves

### Google: Gemini Takes Over Workspace + Maps Gets Its Biggest Upgrade in a Decade

**Workspace (March 10):** Deep Gemini integration across Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drive. The AI can now pull from your files, emails, and Chat history to generate fully structured content. Requires Google AI Ultra subscription.

**Google Maps (March 12):** Gemini-powered **"Ask Maps"** conversational feature + redesigned **Immersive Navigation** with 3D views, accurate overpasses, crosswalks, landmarks, and natural voice guidance.

🔗 [Google Blog](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/workspace/gemini-workspace-updates-march-2026/) | [TechCrunch - Maps](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/12/google-maps-is-getting-an-ai-ask-maps-feature-and-upgraded-immersive-navigation/)

## 🎯 Takeaways

1. **Multi-model is the new default.** Microsoft running OpenAI + Anthropic side-by-side in Copilot makes it official: no single model provider owns the enterprise AI stack. The orchestration layer wins.

2. **Anthropic's enterprise moat is deepening fast.** 70% win rate + $100M partner network + certification program = ecosystem play. But the Pentagon episode shows that values-based positioning cuts both ways.

3. **Google's "Gemini everywhere" execution is impressive.** Workspace integration + Maps overhaul in the same week. Gemini is becoming the operating system beneath Google's product surface.

4. **Eyes on GTC next week.** NVIDIA's developer conference opens Monday. With Jensen hinting at the end of AI company investments and competitors circling, expect this to be the month's most consequential hardware/infra event.
