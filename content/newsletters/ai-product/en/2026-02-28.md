## 🏆 Cover Story: OpenAI Closes $110B Round — The Largest Private Funding in History

OpenAI announced on February 27 that it has raised **$110 billion** at a valuation of $730-840B. Amazon led with $50B, followed by Nvidia and SoftBank at $30B each. This isn't just the largest AI funding round — it's the largest private funding round ever.

The same day, [Microsoft and OpenAI issued a joint statement](https://blogs.microsoft.com/blog/2026/02/27/microsoft-and-openai-joint-statement-on-continuing-partnership/) reaffirming their partnership. Revenue-sharing terms remain unchanged, but OpenAI can now work with other cloud providers — meaning Amazon is now both investor and compute supplier. The AI arms race has entered its "everyone vs. Google" phase.

## 🔥 This Week's Big Moves

### Anthropic Launches Claude Cowork, Takes Aim at Microsoft 365 Copilot
On February 24, Anthropic unveiled **"Cowork & Plugins for the Enterprise"** — embedding Claude directly inside Excel, PowerPoint, and Slack. Not as a separate chat window, but as an integrated layer that carries context across applications.

Stock plug-ins ship for finance, legal, and HR departments, all customizable. Anthropic's pitch: "In 2025, Claude Code transformed how software gets built. In 2026, we're bringing that transformation to all knowledge work."

This is the most direct challenge yet to Microsoft Copilot and OpenAI's Frontier.

📎 [Business Insider](https://www.businessinsider.com/anthropic-ai-software-claude-microsoft-powerpoint-excel-slack-2026-2) · [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/24/anthropic-launches-new-push-for-enterprise-agents-with-plugins-for-finance-engineering-and-design/) · [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/anthropic-says-claude-code-transformed-programming-now-claude-cowork-is)

### Google Gemini Now Books Rides and Orders Food on Android
On February 25, Google launched Gemini's multi-step autonomous task capability on Android (beta). Long-press the power button, ask Gemini to book an Uber or reorder your last DoorDash meal, and it handles the rest. The AI assistant is finally graduating from "answering questions" to "running errands."

📎 [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/25/gemini-can-now-automate-some-multi-step-tasks-on-android/) · [Google Blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/products/gemini-app/android-multi-step-tasks/)

### Seven Tech Giants to Sign White House AI Energy Pact
Amazon, Google, Meta, Microsoft, xAI, Oracle, and OpenAI will sign an agreement next week at the White House to build their own electricity supply network for AI data centers. AI's hunger for compute is now reshaping the energy landscape.

## 🎯 Takeaways

1. **Capital is accelerating**: OpenAI $110B + Anthropic $30B + India summit $200B+ — over $340B in AI capital flow in a single week. This isn't bubble talk; this is infrastructure-level commitment.

2. **Enterprise is the real battleground**: Anthropic Cowork, Microsoft Copilot, OpenAI Frontier — three companies going all-in on the digital workplace simultaneously. The 2026 AI race isn't about model benchmarks; it's about who owns your Excel and Slack.

3. **Agents are shipping**: Google Gemini booking rides, Claude running analyses inside your spreadsheet — AI agents went from demos to products this week.

4. **Energy is AI's hard constraint**: Seven tech giants jointly building power infrastructure signals that AI's bottleneck has shifted from algorithms to physics. Control energy, control AI scale.

5. **SaaS is being repriced**: When AI can directly replace SaaS functionality, the entire software industry's valuation framework needs rewriting. This isn't a future risk — it's happening now.
