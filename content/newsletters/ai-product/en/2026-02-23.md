## 🏆 Cover Story: NVIDIA's $30B Bet on OpenAI Signals a New Phase in the AI Arms Race

NVIDIA is close to finalizing a **$30 billion investment** in OpenAI, replacing an earlier $100B infrastructure deal that fell through. The investment is part of a funding round that could exceed $100B total, valuing OpenAI at approximately **$850 billion**. SoftBank and Amazon are also in discussions.

The chipmaker taking a direct equity stake in its largest customer marks a deepening of the AI value chain's vertical integration. With OpenAI planning to spend $600B on compute infrastructure, this is no longer a product race—it's an infrastructure race.

## 🔥 This Week's Headlines

### 1. OpenAI Launches Ads in ChatGPT
Starting February 9, OpenAI began testing ads for Free and Go ($8/mo) tier users in the US. Sponsored placements from brands like Expedia appear after a user's very first prompt. Plus/Pro/Enterprise remain ad-free. Free users can opt out of ads in exchange for fewer daily messages.

This is ChatGPT's monetization inflection point—800M users are now an ad surface.

📎 [WinBuzzer](https://winbuzzer.com/2026/02/21/chatgpt-ads-now-appearing-first-prompt-free-users-openai-xcxwbn/) · [ContentGrip](https://www.contentgrip.com/openai-chatgpt-ads-rollout/)

### 2. Anthropic's Super Bowl Ad Gambit Pays Off: Claude Users Up 11%
Anthropic ran two Super Bowl LX commercials as part of its "A Time and a Place" campaign—parodying AI assistants that suddenly pivot to hawking products mid-conversation. The tagline: **"Ads are coming to AI. But not to Claude."**

Results: **11% DAU increase** for Claude, App Store rank jumped to #7, downloads up 32%. ChatGPT saw only 2.7% DAU growth, Gemini 1.4%. Sam Altman called the ads "deceptive." The rivalry has officially moved from product to brand warfare.

📎 [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2026/02/19/openai-anthropic-sam-altman-dario-amodei-refused-to-hold-hands-ai-super-bowl-ad-war-ceos-big-tech-conflict/) · [eMarketer](https://www.emarketer.com/content/claude-users-grow-11--on-back-of-super-bowl-spot-anthropic-rides-wave-of-anti-ai-ads-sentiment)

### 3. OpenAI Retires GPT-4o and Legacy Models; 22K Sign Petition
On February 13, OpenAI officially retired GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, GPT-4.1 mini, o4-mini, and GPT-5 (Instant/Thinking) from ChatGPT. Over **22,000 users signed a petition** to save GPT-4o, citing its conversational warmth and emotional resonance. API access remains unchanged for now.

📎 [Business Insider](https://www.businessinsider.com/openai-retires-gpt-4o-20-000-sign-petition-save-it-2026-2) · [Dataconomy](https://dataconomy.com/2026/02/16/openai-officially-retires-gpt-4o-and-legacy-models-from-chatgpt/)

### 4. India AI Impact Summit: $260B+ in Investment Pledges
India hosted its AI Impact Summit (Feb 16-19) in New Delhi, with 37 CEOs including Altman, Amodei, Pichai, Hassabis, and Ambani. PM Modi framed AI as a "civilizational turning point."

Key outcomes:
- **Reliance + Adani pledged a combined $210B** for domestic AI and data infrastructure
- **OpenAI partnered with TCS** to deploy 100MW of AI compute (targeting 1GW)
- **Google announced a $15B AI Hub** and research partnerships in India
- India aims to attract **$200B in AI investment** over two years

Bonus: During the group photo, Modi asked all leaders to hold hands. Altman and Amodei pointedly refused to hold each other's—the Super Bowl ad war made flesh. 😂

## 🤖 Model & API Updates

### OpenAI Ships Lockdown Mode for Enterprise
A new security feature targeting high-risk enterprise users to mitigate **prompt injection attacks**. When enabled:
- Web browsing restricted to cached content (no live network requests)
- Deep Research and Agent Mode disabled
- Canvas-generated code cannot access the network
- Responses cannot include images

Available for Enterprise/Edu/Healthcare/Teachers plans. Also introduced **"Elevated Risk" labels** for high-risk capabilities.

📎 [ZDNet](https://www.zdnet.com/article/how-chatgpt-lockdown-mode-protects-prompt-injection-attacks/) · [Help Net Security](https://www.helpnetsecurity.com/2026/02/16/chatgpt-lockdown-mode-elevated-risk/)

### Big Tech AI CapEx Hits $650B+
2026 capital expenditure guidance from the hyperscalers:
- **Amazon**: $200B
