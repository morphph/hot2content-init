## 🏆 Cover Story: The AI Arms Race Enters the Hundred-Billion-Dollar Era

This week's defining theme: **capital at unprecedented scale**. OpenAI is closing a $100B funding round at an $850B valuation — with NVIDIA alone putting in $30B. Anthropic just wrapped a $30B Series G at $380B. Meta's 2026 AI budget: $135B. These aren't just big checks — they're reshaping the capital structure of the entire tech industry.

Meanwhile, OpenAI quietly revised its 2030 compute target from $1.4T to $600B — a signal that IPO preparation is in full swing. Not a retreat; a pivot from pitch deck mode to S-1 mode.

## 🔥 Top Stories

### 1. OpenAI Acquires OpenClaw — Personal AI Agent Strategy Takes Shape
On Feb 15, Sam Altman announced that Peter Steinberger, creator of OpenClaw — the fastest-growing open-source AI agent tool — is joining OpenAI to lead next-gen personal AI agent development. The message is clear: **the ChatGPT era is winding down; the Agent era begins now.**
→ [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/openais-acquisition-of-openclaw-signals-the-beginning-of-the-end-of-the) · [Leanware Analysis](https://www.leanware.co/insights/openai-openclaw-acquisition)

### 2. NVIDIA's $30B Investment in OpenAI
NVIDIA is finalizing a $30B stake in OpenAI as part of a mega-round that includes Amazon, SoftBank, and Microsoft. Total round could exceed $100B at an $850B valuation. The chip supplier is now the equity partner — supply chains are becoming capital chains.
→ [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/business/nvidia-close-finalizing-30-billion-investment-openai-funding-round-ft-reports-2026-02-20/) · [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/19/openai-reportedly-finalizing-100b-deal-at-more-than-850b-valuation/)

### 3. Anthropic Closes $30B Series G at $380B Valuation
Led by GIC and Coatue, with D.E. Shaw, Dragoneer, and Founders Fund participating. Second-largest private financing in tech history (after OpenAI's $40B). Monthly revenue hit $1.16B in February.
→ [AI Business](https://aibusiness.com/generative-ai/anthropic-raises-30b-bringing-valuation-to-380b)

## 🤖 Model & API Updates

### Anthropic Ships Claude Sonnet 4.6
Released February 17. Key highlights:
- **1M token context window** (2× previous Sonnet max)
- Performance approaching Opus 4.5 at Sonnet pricing ($3/$15 per M tokens)
- Significant improvements in coding, computer use, agent planning, and design
- Now the default model for Free and Pro users

The value proposition is striking: near-flagship performance at mid-tier prices. For developers, this effectively doubles the cost-performance ratio overnight.
→ [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/17/anthropic-releases-sonnet-4-6/) · [Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Feb/17/claude-sonnet-46/) · [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/17/anthropic-ai-claude-sonnet-4-6-default-free-pro.html)

## 🏢 Platform Moves

### Apple Accelerates Three AI Wearables
Bloomberg reports Apple is ramping development on AI-powered smart glasses, a pendant device, and camera-equipped AirPods. Direct competition with Meta Ray-Bans and OpenAI's personal AI hardware vision. Apple's strategy: **skip the infrastructure spending war; differentiate at the device layer.**
→ [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-17/apple-ramps-up-work-on-glasses-pendant-and-camera-airpods-for-ai-era) · [CNET](https://www.cnet.com/tech/mobile/apples-trio-of-ai-wearables-could-arrive-as-soon-as-next-year/)

### Meta Expands NVIDIA Partnership + $135B AI Budget
Meta signed a multi-billion-dollar expansion with NVIDIA for AI chips and data center build-out. 2026 AI spending: $135B. Separately, Meta launched a $65M political campaign backing AI-friendly state politicians ahead of the midterms, starting in Texas and Illinois.
→ [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/17/meta-nvidia-deal-ai-data-center-chips.html) · [NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/02/18/technology/meta-65-million-election-ai.html)

### Google Announces I/O 2026 Date + AI Music Features
Google confirmed the I/O 2026 developer conference date. Both Google and Apple are adding generative AI music creation to core consumer apps — a milestone for mainstream AI adoption.
→ [Business Standard](https://www.business-standard.com/technology/tech-news/tech-wrap-feb-18-google-i-o-2026-date-announced-apple-ai-devices-antheopic-claude-sonnet-4-6-google-pixel-10a-ios-26-4-public-beta-126021800987_1.html) · [LA Times](https://www.latimes.com/business/story/2026-02-18/google-apple-bring-ai-music-creation-to-mainstream-consumers)

## 📣 Regulation & Politics

### Anthropic vs. The Pentagon: AI Safety Meets National Defense
The most dramatic standoff this week. Anthropic deployed Claude on Pentagon classified systems under a $200M contract — but negotiations over AI use in surveillance and weapons systems have broken down. The DoD is threatening to label Anthropic a "supply chain risk." Safety-first AI company vs. the world's largest military — this tension will define 2026.
→ [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/16/anthropic-defense-department-relationship-hegseth) · [NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/02/18/technology/defense-department-anthropic-ai-safety.html) · [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/technology/2026/02/22/pentagon-anthropic-ai-dispute/)

## 🎯 Takeaways

1. **Agents over Chatbots.** OpenAI's OpenClaw acquisition is the week's most telling signal. They didn't buy technology — they bought a direction. Personal AI agents are the next battleground.

2. **Capital is rewriting the rules.** $100B funding rounds, $600B compute budgets, $135B annual spend — AI is no longer a tech sector vertical. It's restructuring global capital markets.

3. **The safety-versus-deployment tension is escalating.** Anthropic vs. Pentagon, Meta's political lobbying, $150M in election spending — AI companies face increasingly difficult choices between growth and principles.

4. **Sonnet 4.6's price-performance logic.** Near-flagship capability at mid-tier pricing is what actually drives AI adoption. Developers now get last quarter's $5/M performance for $3/M.
