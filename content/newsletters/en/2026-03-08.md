## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is "post-training" and why does it matter?** Post-training is the phase after a model's initial pre-training on raw text. It includes techniques like RLHF (reinforcement learning from human feedback) and instruction tuning that transform a raw text predictor into something that actually follows instructions, reasons through problems, and refuses harmful requests. It's the difference between a model that can complete sentences and one that can hold a useful conversation. When OpenAI's post-training lead moves to Anthropic, he's bringing the expertise that makes models *useful*, not just *capable*.
