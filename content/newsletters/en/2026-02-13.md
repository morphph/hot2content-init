**What is knowledge distillation?**  
When a large, capable model (the "teacher") helps train a smaller model (the "student") by sharing its outputs rather than raw training data. The student learns to mimic the teacher's behavior, often achieving surprising capability at a fraction of the compute cost. Today's Upskill project is a twist on this: Claude writes specialized code, and that code becomes training signal for open models. It's how proprietary capabilities might eventually leak into the open ecosystemâ€”not through weights, but through outputs.
