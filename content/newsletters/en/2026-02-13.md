**What is "infrastructure noise" in benchmarks?**  
When researchers test AI models, the results depend not just on the model but on how the test environment is configured‚Äîthings like timeout settings, retry logic, and hardware specs. Anthropic's new research shows these factors can swing scores by several percentage points. This matters because leaderboard rankings often separate models by just 1-2 points. A model that "wins" might simply have been tested under more favorable conditions.
