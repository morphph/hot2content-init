**What is "server-side compaction"?**  
When AI agents run for extended periods, their conversation history grows until it exceeds the model's context windowâ€”the maximum amount of text it can process at once. Server-side compaction automatically summarizes older parts of the conversation, preserving essential information while freeing up space for new context. This lets agents maintain coherent multi-hour sessions without losing track of what they're doing, a critical capability for autonomous coding and research tasks.
