## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is async scheduling in LLM inference?** Traditional LLM serving processes requests sequentially — one batch finishes before the next starts. Async scheduling lets the system overlap computation: while one batch is generating tokens, another can be prefilling prompts. Combined with pipeline parallelism (splitting model layers across GPUs), this eliminates idle time and explains vLLM's 30% speedup. It's the difference between a single-lane road and a highway interchange.
