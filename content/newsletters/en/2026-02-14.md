## üéì MODEL LITERACY

**What's a "full-duplex" conversational model?** Traditional voice AI uses a pipeline: your speech is transcribed (ASR), sent to an LLM, then synthesized back to audio (TTS). This forces rigid turn-taking ‚Äî the model can't interrupt or respond while you're talking. Full-duplex models like MiniCPM-o 4.5 and PersonaPlex-7B process audio directly in both directions simultaneously, enabling natural interruptions and overlapping speech, just like human conversation. The architecture skips text entirely, treating voice as a native modality.
