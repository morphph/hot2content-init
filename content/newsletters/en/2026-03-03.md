## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What's a KV-cache and why does LMCache matter?**  
When LLMs process text, they compute "key-value" pairs representing attention between tokens. These computations are expensive — but identical prompts produce identical KV pairs. LMCache recognizes when your model is recomputing work it's already done (common in RAG systems, long contexts, and repeated prompts) and reuses the cached results instead. That's how it saves enterprises 50%+ of inference compute without changing model output. Google Cloud, CoreWeave, and NVIDIA use it in production for exactly this reason.
