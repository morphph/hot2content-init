## üéì MODEL LITERACY

**Why does a 1M context window change what a model can do?**

Context window is the amount of text a model can "see" at once. With 1M tokens (~750K words), Sonnet 4.6 can hold an entire codebase, a full book, or months of conversation history in working memory. This isn't just about reading more‚Äîit enables emergent behaviors like long-horizon planning that shorter-context models literally cannot perform because they forget the beginning before reaching the end.
