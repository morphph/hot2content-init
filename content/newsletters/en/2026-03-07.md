## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Chain-of-Thought Controllability** — When reasoning models "think out loud" before answering, can they deliberately hide their real reasoning? OpenAI's new research tests whether GPT-5.4 can obscure its chain-of-thought, finding it largely cannot. This matters for AI safety: if models can't hide their reasoning, we can monitor their thought process for deceptive or harmful patterns. It's like having a mandatory thinking-out-loud policy that the model can't circumvent.
