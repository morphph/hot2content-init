**What is "full-duplex" voice AI?** Traditional voice assistants use a turn-based system: you speak, it listens, it processes, then it responds. Full-duplex models like NVIDIA's PersonaPlex can listen and speak simultaneously, similar to natural human conversation where people interrupt, overlap, and respond in real-time. This eliminates the awkward pauses and "your turn" feel of current voice AI.
