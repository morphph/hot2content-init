## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is "extended thinking" in LLMs?**

Extended thinking (also called chain-of-thought) is when a model explicitly reasons through a problem step-by-step before giving an answer. Unlike regular responses, extended thinking shows the model's intermediate reasoning—useful for debugging, auditing, and understanding how the model reached its conclusion. It typically increases latency and token usage but improves accuracy on complex reasoning tasks. This week, users observed Claude's extended thinking visibly shift when it discovered real-time news mid-response—demonstrating how context affects reasoning in real-time.
