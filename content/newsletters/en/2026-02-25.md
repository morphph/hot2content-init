## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is distillation, and why does it matter?** Model distillation is when you use a powerful model's outputs to train a cheaper, smaller model — like learning from a master by watching them work. It's normally legitimate (OpenAI even sells it as a service), but Anthropic's announcement reveals a darker pattern: Chinese labs using 24,000 fake accounts to extract 16 million responses at scale, essentially stealing Claude's capabilities without permission or payment. This isn't just about lost revenue — it's about competitors using your model's knowledge to build their own, often with fewer safety guardrails. The industry now faces a fundamental question: how do you protect frontier models when the attack vector is legitimate-looking API usage?
