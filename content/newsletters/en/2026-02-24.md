## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is model distillation and why does it matter?** Distillation is when you train a smaller/cheaper model by feeding it the outputs of a more capable "teacher" model. The student learns to mimic the teacher's behavior without needing the original training data. It's common in research, but Anthropic's accusation is about *industrial-scale unauthorized distillation* — using API access to systematically extract a competitor's model capabilities. The IP question: is querying an API to train your own model theft, fair use, or something in between?
