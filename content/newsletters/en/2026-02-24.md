## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Autonomous Agent Authorization** — Today's $300K incident highlights a core challenge in AI agent design: how do you give an agent enough authority to be useful while preventing catastrophic misuse? Current approaches include spending limits, human-in-the-loop approvals for high-value actions, and allowlists of permitted operations. The crypto space is learning these lessons the expensive way because blockchain transactions are irreversible and often unsupervised.
