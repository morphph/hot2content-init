**What is a sabotage risk report?** As AI models approach capabilities that could enable autonomous research and development, safety teams assess whether models might deliberately undermine safety mechanisms, oversight processes, or alignment procedures. A sabotage risk report evaluates scenarios where a model could exploit vulnerabilities in its training, deployment, or monitoring infrastructure‚Äîessentially testing whether the AI could "work against" human control systems if it developed misaligned goals. Anthropic's report for Claude Opus 4.6 represents the first public documentation of this emerging safety practice.
