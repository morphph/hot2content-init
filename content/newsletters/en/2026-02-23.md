## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is "infrastructure noise" in AI benchmarks?**  
When you run an AI model on a coding task, the results depend not just on the model itself, but on everything around it: the machine's specs, network latency, timeout settings, even which version of Python is installed. Anthropic's research shows this "noise" can shift benchmark scores by several percentage points—enough to flip model rankings on leaderboards. This is why a single benchmark number rarely tells the whole story.
