## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**What is a "one-shot" coding agent?** Traditional AI coding assistants work iteratively—you prompt, they respond, you correct, they try again. A one-shot agent attempts to complete an entire task in a single pass: understand the requirement, plan the solution, write the code, and verify it works. Stripe's Minions architecture shows this isn't just theoretical—it's running in production at one of tech's most demanding engineering organizations. The challenge is reliability: one-shot only works when the model is confident enough to commit without human checkpoints.
