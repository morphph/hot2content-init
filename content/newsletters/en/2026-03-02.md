## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Why Anthropic and OpenAI's military stances matter for developers**

This week's divergence isn't just corporate PR. Anthropic's Dario Amodei publicly refused mass surveillance and autonomous weapons contracts with the Department of War. OpenAI signed a deal to deploy AI in DoD classified environments the next day. For developers, this means the models you build on may have different operational constraints depending on use case. Anthropic's Claude will likely have harder guardrails against certain applications; OpenAI's models may have more flexibility but also more scrutiny. Choose your foundation model with eyes open about where the parent company draws lines.
